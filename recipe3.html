Mac and Cheese

Mac and Cheese "is a pasta dish of macaroni covered in cheese sauce, most commonly cheddar sauce. Its origins trace back to cheese and pasta casseroles in medieval England. The traditional macaroni and cheese is put in a casserole dish and baked in the oven; however, it may be prepared in a sauce pan on top of the stove, sometimes using a packaged mix such as became popular in the mid-20th century,"Wikipedia.

Mac and Cheese is a pasta called macaronies that you put in boiling water, remove the water, then add milk, and cheese.

Learn more

Things needed

Use whatever cheeses you want

  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Macaroni
  • A pot
  • Water
  • Stove

Recipe

Speprate water using lid of the pot

  1. Put water in a pot
  2. Put pot on stove and boil the water
  3. Add Macaronies to pot
  4. Wait until Mac is soft (Around 5-8 mins)
  5. Remove water from pot
  6. Add milk and cheese to pot
  7. Mix it up till ready!

Then you have the Mac and Cheese
